Cupcakes

Cupcakes are versatile and you can turn literally anything into a cupcake.

Here's a recipe for Pickle Cupcakes

  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 2 Flour
  • 1/2tsp Baking powder
  • 2 Cups Sugar
  • 1 Butter
  • 1/2 Cup Pick Juice
  • 2 Cups Cream Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup Whipped Cream
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup Pickle Juice
  • 1/2 Cup Diced Pickle
  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°
  2. Mix dry ingredients
  3. Cream butter and sugar in a separate bowl
  4. Mixed together the dry ingredients with the butter and sugar mixture
  5. Scoop the batter into your cupcake mould
  6. For the icing, whisk the whipped cream to a soft peak
  7. Mix in the cream cheese and sugar
  8. Piped the icing on the cooled down cupcakes
  9. Sprinkled the diced pickles on top
